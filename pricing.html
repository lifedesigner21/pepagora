Frequently Asked Questions

01

Plan & Account Basics

What is the main difference between "Foundation" and "Growth" plans?
Foundation is a basic plan, while Growth includes more advanced tools to help you grow your business faster.
I'm not sure which plan is right for my business. Can you help?
Yes! You can start with the Free plan and upgrade as your business grows.
Can I really use all plans for both sourcing and selling?
Yes, absolutely. Every Pepagora account is a unified business account. You can post buy requirements and search for suppliers on every plan. The Growth plans simply add more powerful tools to help you proactively market and sell your own products.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime based on your needs.
Do you offer a free trial for the Growth plans?
We do offer limited-time trials during special promotions. Stay tuned for updates.
Are there limits on products, contacts, or buy requirements?
Some limits may apply depending on your plan. Growth plans typically offer higher limits.
02

Billing & Subscription

How does the billing cycle work?
Billing is processed yearly for paid plans, starting from the date of purchase.
Can I get an invoice?
Yes, invoices are automatically emailed after payment.
03

Global & International Business

Can I sell internationally?
Yes, the platform supports global buyers and sellers.
04

Features, Security & Support

How secure is my data?
We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data.
